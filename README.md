# Jay Gutierrez, PhD

**Computational systems scientist · Graph intelligence · Network science · Natural capital**

I build graph-based intelligence systems that reveal structural dynamics, emergent risks, and hidden dependencies invisible to aggregate metrics. My work spans knowledge graph architecture, ecological network modeling, gene regulatory networks, blue carbon verification, and natural capital assessment — across biological, ecological, and decentralized systems.

---

### What I build

- **Knowledge graphs at scale** — production systems with 30M+ nodes and 170M+ relationships, encoded with domain semantics and queried via Cypher and GraphRAG
- **Ecological network intelligence** — food web reconstruction, k-core fragility analysis, cascade modeling, and biodiversity risk quantification across 11,928 species and 105 empirical food webs
- **Natural capital baselines** — investor-grade geospatial assessments integrating multi-sensor remote sensing (Sentinel-2, Landsat, SAR), CatBoost carbon stock modeling (R²=0.926), and Monte Carlo uncertainty at national scale (276K+ ha, 10K+ planning units)
- **Nature-finance translation** — Bio-VaR (Biodiversity Value-at-Risk), bond-style MPA ratings (AAA–CCC), ecosystem service valuation frameworks, and TNFD/ISSB-aligned biodiversity risk metrics for insurance and capital markets
- **Blue carbon verification** — VM0033/VCS-aligned pipeline with multi-GHG accounting via molecular markers (mcrA/pmoA), blue carbon site intelligence for community-led projects across Colombia and Ecuador (440K+ ha)
- **Gene regulatory networks** — GRN inference and analysis from single-cell data, community graph pipelines, and population structure visualization
- **Agentic graph pipelines** — multi-agent systems with LangGraph and GraphRAG for graph-grounded reasoning and discovery
